ABOUT NEVER DANGEROUS

Our team is going to create a website similar to the crime map, called "NEVER DANGEROUS", to introduce the audience to crime situation in Queensland. The datasets we chose shows the crime rate and victim numbers on districts around Queensland. Our purpose is to provide effective crime information for the audience through this website, and give the caution and prevent the happening of the danger.

TARGET AUDIENCE

The target audience including but not limited to:

  • Students
  • Byers
  • Tenants
